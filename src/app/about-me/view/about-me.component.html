About Me

Hello, My name is Nathan! I am a second-year Information Technology student at the University of Cincinnati. I am pursuing my BS in Information Technology with a focus on Software Application Development. I will also graduate with my Master of Business Administration thanks to the prestigious University of Cincinnati IT Accelerated Program. While completing my MBA I will learn broad business concepts from an IT perspective and apply that specialized view to problem-solving, communication, and hands-on skills. I will also receive a minor in Computer Science. With this, I will enhance my theoretical knowledge and dive deep into the algorithms that drive today's computers.

I have been invested in technology my entire life. My dad worked in IT so I was always in and out of his work at a young age exploring the server room and different wiring closets. I started taking different engineering classes at the age of 12 and it wasn't until my sophomore year of high school that I fell in love with coding. From that point on I took every computer science class available and would work on personal coding projects. Now at the University of Cincinnati, I can do what I love every day and expand my knowledge of today's technology.
