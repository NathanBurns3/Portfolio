About Me

I'm a second-year Information Technology student at the University of Cincinnati. I am pursuing my BS in Information Technology with a focus on Software Application Development. Additionally, I have the opportunity to graduate with a Master of Business Administration through the esteemed University of Cincinnati IT Accelerated Program. While completing my MBA I will learn broad business concepts from an IT perspective and apply that specialized view to problem-solving, communication, and hands-on skills. Complementing my studies, I will also obtain a minor in Computer Science to further enhance my theoretical knowledge and dive deep into the algorithms that drive today's computers.

Ever since I can remember, I've been deeply invested in technology. My father's work in IT introduced me to various areas within his office at a young age. At the age of 12, I began taking engineering classes, but it wasn't until my sophomore year of high school that I truly fell in love with coding. Since then, I have enrolled in every computer science class available and continuously pursued personal projects. Now at the University of Cincinnati, I can do what I love every day and expand my knowledge of today's technology.

During the past summer, I had the valuable opportunity to work as a QA Software Tester Co-op at London Computer Systems. In this role, I designed unique test cases to evaluate various aspects of the company's property management software, Rent Manager. Through this experience, I expanded my technical knowledge and gained a profound understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC).
