About Me

I'm a third-year Information Technology student at the University of Cincinnati. I am pursuing my BS in Information Technology with a focus on Software Application Development. Additionally, I have the opportunity to graduate with a Master of Business Administration through the esteemed University of Cincinnati IT Accelerated Program. While completing my MBA I will learn broad business concepts from an IT perspective and apply that specialized view to problem-solving, communication, and hands-on skills. Complementing my studies, I will also obtain a minor in Computer Science to further enhance my theoretical knowledge and dive deep into the algorithms that drive today's computers.

Ever since I can remember, I've been deeply invested in technology. My father's work in IT introduced me to various areas within his office at a young age. At the age of 12, I began taking engineering classes, but it wasn't until my sophomore year of high school that I truly fell in love with coding. Since then, I have enrolled in every computer science class available and continuously pursued personal projects. Now at the University of Cincinnati, I can do what I love every day and expand my knowledge of today's technology.

During the summer, I had the valuable opportunity to work as a software developer intern at London Computer Systems. In this role, I contributed to the development of the company's flagship product, Rent Manager Express, a web-based application for property management. I developed on the frontend using the Angular framework, implemented backend functionality using C# and ASP.NET, and utilized MySQL and HeidiSQL for efficient database management.

I was given the privilege to work on many different projects, including updating our 'CAM Reconciliation' page, which is used by thousands of users every day. Property managers will now be able to easily compare CRE with tenant charges, ensuring accurate billing. I was also able to implement a 'Tenant Listbox' page for customers' personal preferences, allowing users the ability to personalize the info displayed in the tenant listboxes.
